ADMISSIONS FACILITIES SPORTS AND ACTIVITIES

Separate criteria exists for the admission of Pre-Nursery, Nursery and KG classes where the age of the child should be between 3 years and 5 years. Children are admitted to these classes on production of birth Certificate issued by the Comptetent Authority and on the basis of an interview of the child together with his/her parents. The interview with the parents is conducted to appriase them in their involvement with their wards at home to ensure better students.

Discover Oaks School provides a balanced environment focused on shaping the children to fit into the world of tomorrow. Noida Public School provides best facilities like smart and spacious class rooms, Library/media, Computer Center, Science Laboratories, Games & Sports, Music, Transport etc.

The school truly believes that for the all round development of the student it is very important to possess a healthy mind in a healthy body. Therefore our school encourages different sports activities with the help of our able and experienced physical instructors we have different indoor and outdoor sports activities like Volleyball, Cricket, Badmintonand Kho- Kho and different indoor games like Chess & carrom.
